About Me

Sherry Zhang

My name is Sherry, and I am a sophomore at Penn State University studying Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics and Cybersecurity Computational Foundations. Some of my interests that I am exploring include full-stack development, cybersecurity, and machine learning.

My interest in full-stack development stemmed from my younger self wanting a personal website. Although my current website is static and mainly features HTML and CSS, I am learning JavaScript and different frameworks and libraries, such as React, Node, and Express, to make it more dynamic in the future. Next, my interest in cybersecurity developed after an unfortunate event where my debit card got hacked back in high school. Last, machine learning is something I recently got into after reading a journal paper last year on a reinforcement learning algorithm developed by CalTech to run on underwater drones.

During my free time, you can find me exploring new parts of the city, hiking in the mountains, eating spicy salmon poke bowls, or watching Star Wars. Most importantly, I enjoy spending time with my friends and family. I also play the violin. I have over eleven years of experience with the instrument, and I used to play for the San Jose Youth Symphony Philharmonic Orchestra back home.
