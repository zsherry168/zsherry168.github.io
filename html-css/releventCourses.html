Mathematics Courses

STAT 414: Introduction to Probability Theory (In progress)

This course incorporates calculus into probability to describe the uncertainties present in real applications. Topics include probability spaces, discrete and continuous random variables, transformations, expectations, generating functions, conditional distributions, law of large numbers, and central limit theorems.

MATH 230: Calculus and Vector Analysis

Three-dimensional analytic geometry, vectors in space, partial differentiations, double and triple integrals, and integral vector calculus.

MATH 220: Matrices

Systems of linear equations, matrix algebra, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, linear systems of differential equations.
